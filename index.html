The Solution

Pricing intelligence
built for the bench,
not the boardroom.

01
Live Metal Risk Engine
Model your portfolio's exposure to gold, silver, platinum, and palladium in real time. Simulate how a 10%, 20%, or 30% metal price move impacts every item's margin — before it happens.
02
Portfolio Volatility Index
A proprietary 0–100 risk score that quantifies your portfolio's exposure to precious metal volatility. Know your risk at a glance, updated every time prices move.
03
Shopify Auto-Repricing
When margins are at risk, Assay generates repricing suggestions and pushes updated prices directly to your Shopify storefront — with built-in safety controls and a full audit trail.
04
Historical Risk Tracking
Every portfolio margin, volatility score, and metal price is recorded over time. Understand how your risk profile has evolved and identify patterns before they become losses.
05
Dynamic Repricing Suggestions
Set a target margin floor. Assay calculates the exact retail price each item needs to maintain profitability under live metal costs — and flags items at loss risk before you sell them.
06
Built for Scale
The engine layer is architected for multi-brand deployment. One platform, multiple brands, each with isolated data and customised risk parameters. Ready for enterprise pilot.
